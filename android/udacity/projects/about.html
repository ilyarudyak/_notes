About

With this app you can search a book in Google project ALEXANDRIA using ISBN-10 or -13 (enter manually or scan). You can also add a book to your library.

Settings

You may choose your start screen - Book Search or Library. You may also choose to use Live search. In this case search will be performed automatically when you enter a valid ISBN. Otherwise you should press Search button manually.

How to scan a book

Just press a scanner icon on Action Bar and use your canera to scan ISBN of a book. When scan is performed you are redirected to standard search screen. You than searh book manually or it will be searched automatically based on your settings.

How to view book details

You may press a book entry in Library screen to drill down to a detail screen. You can also delete a book in this detail view.
